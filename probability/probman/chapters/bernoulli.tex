\item Find the probability of getting a head when a coin is tossed once. Also
find the probability of getting a tail.
\\
\solution  Let the random variable be $X\in \cbrak{0,1}$.  Then
\begin{align}
\pr{X=0}=\pr{X=1}=\frac{1}{2}
\end{align}
